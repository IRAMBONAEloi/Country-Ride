What vehicles can I rent through CRIDE?

CRIDE offers a variety of vehicles, including cars, motorcycles, and bicycles, to suit your travel needs.
Browse our selection on the website to find the perfect ride for your adventure!

How do I make a reservation?

You can easily make a reservation online through our website or by contacting our customer service team.

What are the rental requirements?

You will need a valid driver's license, your ID, a credit card for the deposit, and proof of insurance .

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours in advance are eligible for a full refund. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Are there any age restrictions for renting?

Yes, renters must be at least 21 years old to rent cars and motorcycles. There are no age restrictions for renting bicycles, but minors will need parental or guardian consent.

Do I need insurance to rent a vehicle?

Basic insurance is included, but additional coverage options are available for purchase.

What should I do if I encounter a problem with my rental?

If you encounter a problem with your rental, contact CRIDE customer support immediately. We’ll assist with troubleshooting, repairs, or arrange a replacement if necessary. Emergency contact details are provided in your rental agreement.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can extend your rental period, subject to availability. Contact CRIDE before your rental ends to arrange the extension and make additional payments if needed.

What payment methods are accepted?

CRIDE accepts Mobile payments, major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard.Not yet for American Express

Are there any mileage limits?

Yes, mileage limits may apply depending on the vehicle and rental type. Check your rental agreement for specific details, or ask CRIDE customer support.

Do you provide safety gear with bicycle rentals?

Yes, we provide helmets and locks with all bicycle rentals to ensure your safety and security.
