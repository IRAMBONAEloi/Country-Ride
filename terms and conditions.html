    Welcome to CRIDE. By using our website and services, you agree to these Terms & Conditions. Please read them carefully before renting any vehicles (cars, motorcycles, or bicycles) from us.

    2. Rental Requirements

    Age: You must be at least 21 years old to rent a car or motorcycle. Bicycle rentals are available to all ages, but minors must have parental/guardian consent.
    Driver’s License: A valid driver’s license is required for all car and motorcycle rentals. For motorcycles, a motorcycle endorsement may be needed depending on local.
    Payment: A valid credit/debit card is required for payment and security deposits.

    3. Rental Period & Extensions

    The rental period begins at the time of vehicle pick-up and ends at the agreed return time.
    Extensions must be requested before the rental period ends and are subject to availability. Additional fees may apply.

    4. Cancellations & Refunds

    Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the rental start date are eligible for a full refund.
    Cancellations within 24 hours of the start date may incur a cancellation fee.
    No refunds will be provided for early returns.

    5. Insurance & Liability

    Basic insurance coverage is mandatory and included in your rental. You may choose to purchase additional coverage for further protection.
    You are responsible for any damage, loss, or theft of the vehicle during the rental period, up to the deductible outlined in the rental agreement.
    You agree to indemnify CRIDE for any costs or legal actions arising from your use of the vehicle.

    6. Vehicle Use & Restrictions

    Vehicles must only be used for personal transportation. Commercial use, racing, or illegal activities are strictly prohibited.
    Renters are responsible for following local traffic laws. Any fines, tickets, or penalties incurred during the rental period are the renter’s responsibility.

    7. Return Policy

    Vehicles must be returned on time, with the agreed fuel level, and in a clean condition.
    Additional charges may apply for late returns, excessive wear, or damage.
    If the vehicle is returned after the scheduled time without prior notice, CRIDE reserves the right
    to charge late fees or additional rental charges.

    8. Security Deposit

    A refundable security deposit is required at the time of rental. The deposit will be refunded after
    the vehicle is returned in good condition, minus any charges for damages, late fees,
    or violations.

    9. Mileage Limits

    Mileage limits may apply depending on the vehicle type. Any additional miles driven beyond the
    limit will be subject to extra charges as outlined in the rental agreement.

    10. Breakdown & Accidents
